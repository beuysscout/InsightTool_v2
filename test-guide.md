# Mobile Checkout Research Guide

## Introduction (0:00–5:00)
1. Can you tell me about yourself and your online shopping habits?
2. How often do you shop on mobile?
   - Probe: What apps or sites do you use most?

## Checkout Experience (5:00–20:00)
1. Walk me through the last time you completed a purchase on your phone.
2. What frustrations did you encounter during checkout?
   - Probe: Were there any steps that felt unnecessary?
   - Probe: How did form fields feel on mobile?
3. How did the payment step feel?
   - Probe: Did you use a digital wallet like Apple Pay or Google Pay?

## Post-Purchase (20:00–25:00)
1. What happened after you completed the purchase?
2. How did you feel about the confirmation experience?

## Wrap-up (25:00–30:00)
1. If you could change one thing about mobile checkout, what would it be?
2. Is there anything else you'd like to share?
